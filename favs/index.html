    Websites

    This is a small collection of peoples personal sites that I’ve drawn inspiration from in the past, or admire greatly!

    • Derek Sivers, who’s site was one of my early introductions to “the indie web.”
    • Jack, a very cool designer/artist guy who’s doing a great job creating a small community.
    • myklemorphosis, a very cool and unique site, but which now seems to be under maintenance or deprecated. Check it out on the waybackmachine.
    • ivysly a really interesting game developer with a unique style.
    • medg, especially their poster project. It’s an unending source of inspiration, and the kind of thing I want to undertake.
    • mogdogblog, creator of nubbys number factory, and a site I really enjoy the vibe of.
    • Bill Wurtz, maybe the first personal site I ever visited, and a joy to explore.
    • jvscholz, a guy with a pretty interesting productivity-focused youtube channel, and a site with lots of depth and useful information.
